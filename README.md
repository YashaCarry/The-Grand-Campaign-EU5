The Grand Campaign is a total conversion mod for Europa Universalis V that seeks to stimulate the Victorian era! Although we are not currently close to done, we are making rapid progress on all fronts. In the immediate period, our goal is to release an alpha version with ported borders, pops, economics, and politics. In the long run, we hope to push EU5 and its newer features to their breaking point for the ultimate experience.
